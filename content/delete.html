Delete your FlickTo data

Two ways to remove the data FlickTo holds for you.

If you still have the app (recommended)

This removes everything, including your private encrypted backup, instantly:

  • Open FlickTo.
  • Go to Settings → Social Profile.
  • Tap Delete my social data and confirm.

This removes your social identity, friend card, feed, inbox, encrypted identity backup, and synced friends list from our relay, and clears the data stored on your device. Service credentials (Plex, Trakt, etc.) live only on your device and are gone when you clear app data or uninstall.

If you no longer have the app

Enter your friend code (the short code from your friend card) to remove your public social data from our relay — friend card, profile feed, opinions, and inbox.

Your private end-to-end-encrypted backup is keyed by a secret only you hold (your recovery code) and is not removed by this form — that protects you from anyone else wiping your account. To remove it too, reinstall the app and use the in-app deletion above, or email privacy@flickto.app.
