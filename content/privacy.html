FlickTo Privacy Policy

Effective 3 June 2026

FlickTo ("the app", "we") is a media-discovery app that helps you find films and shows, optionally connecting to services you already use (Plex, Trakt, Sonarr, Radarr, Tautulli, Jellyfin) and to friends. This policy explains what data the app handles, why, and the choices you have.

Data we handle

DataWhyNotes
Advertising IDTo show ads in the Discover screen Provided by Google AdMob. In the EEA/UK we ask for consent before any ad loads.
Crash logs & diagnosticsTo find and fix bugs Firebase Crashlytics and Performance Monitoring. Can be turned off in Settings.
App-interaction analyticsTo understand which features are used Firebase Analytics. Can be turned off in Settings.
Device & installation identifiersPush notifications and analytics Firebase Cloud Messaging token and installation ID.
Social content (display name, avatar choice, opinions/comments, shared lists) The optional friends feature End-to-end encrypted. Our relay stores only ciphertext and opaque tokens — it cannot read your content or see who your friends are.
Service credentials (Plex/Trakt tokens, Sonarr/Radarr/Tautulli URLs & keys, AI keys) To connect to the services you choose Encrypted at rest on your device using the Android Keystore. Plex, Sonarr, Radarr, Tautulli and Jellyfin are self-hosted: the app connects directly to the address you enter (often on your local network), so those credentials are never sent to us or through our proxy. We never receive your service credentials.
IP addressInherent to any network request Seen by us and by the third parties below when the app makes a request.
CameraScanning a friend QR code Processed on-device only. Images are never stored or transmitted.
Location—Not collected. The app holds no location permission.

Third parties

  • Google — AdMob (ads), Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics, Performance, and Cloud Messaging.
  • The services you connect — Plex, Trakt, Sonarr, Radarr, Tautulli, Jellyfin, TMDB. The app talks to these on your behalf using the credentials you enter.
  • Our content backend (Cloudflare) — serves catalog/daily content, the recommendation scoring service, and the zero-knowledge social relay. It also proxies third-party content APIs (Trakt catalog, TMDB, OMDb, JustWatch, Fanart) using FlickTo's own app-level API keys; your personal service credentials do not pass through it.

Encryption

Service credentials and your social identity keys are encrypted on your device with a key held in the hardware-backed Android Keystore. Social content shared with friends is end-to-end encrypted before it leaves your device; the relay only ever stores ciphertext.

Your choices

  • Analytics & diagnostics: toggle off in Settings → Privacy.
  • Ads consent (EEA/UK): shown on first launch and changeable later.
  • Delete your data: see below.

Deleting your data

In the app: Settings → Social Profile → Delete my social data removes your social identity, friend card, feed, inbox, encrypted backup, and synced friends list from the relay, and clears it locally. If you no longer have the app installed, use the web form at flickto.app/delete. Analytics and crash data are deleted by Google on the retention schedule of their respective Firebase products.

Children

FlickTo is not directed at children and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy; the effective date above reflects the latest version.
